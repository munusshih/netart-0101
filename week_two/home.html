What "Taiwanese" Are We Talking About?

Designed and Coded by Munus Shih, Special Thanks to Weikai Cheng

Taiwan is a set of islands in East Asia surrounded by the Philippines, Japan, South Korea, and China. While there is an overemphasis on its relationship with China, Taiwan has its own intricate history. With the majority of the people being immigrants and a history of colonization by the Spanish, the Deutch, the Chinese, and the Japanese, the identity crisis of who gets to be called a "Taiwanese" has always been debatable.


On this website, you will have to answer several questions about what "Taiwnaese" are you referring to when you use the term. You get to leap into the complex history and politics of Taiwan while knowing more about these islands that the Deutch once called "Formosa", meaning beautiful islands.
